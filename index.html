Who We Are

Let's grow your business together

Aarohiinfo, an Indian Strategic Micro-Finance Lending Company serving with the objective to fulfil the purpose of Financial Inclusion to bottom of primite (BOP) segment. We are making financial resources available to the BOP Segment having Businesses to create more wealth through our M-FinTech Platform.

Our endeavour is to bridge the gap between financial sources and the needy section. We help banks and other credit lenders to form a risk free loan portfolio. Our presence in the field and artificial intelligence tools allows us to extend our hands to customers which includes borrowers as well as lenders like banks and other financial institues to smoothen the financial inclusion process. We aim to use the credit to create assets rather than to create a liability.
